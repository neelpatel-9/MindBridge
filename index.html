PG & Hostel Students

Living away from family support systems for the first time. Lack of direct parental oversight makes them highly dependent on digital and peer support channels.

  • High correlation with late-night anxiety spikes
  • Requires 24/7 support availability
Engineering & Medical Cohorts

Rigid curriculums, frequent exams, and placement pressures. Highly competitive grading loops create extreme stress cycles.

  • Academic failure triggers major crisis risks
  • Responds best to structured CBT exercises
